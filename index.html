🎸 How to Play
🎯 Your Goal

You're a talent booker at a dive bar. Put on great shows each week, grow your reputation, and eventually upgrade your venue.

📅 The Week

Each week runs Monday → Saturday. Press Next Day → to advance.

  • 📱 BandBook — Browse acts and send enquiries. They reply within 1–2 days.
  • 📬 Inbox — When a band replies, their offer appears here with negotiation options.
  • 🏠 Office — See the week schedule and pick a mid-week promotion to boost attendance.
  • 🎛️ SFX Gear — Buy and activate production gear to enhance your shows.
  • 🎤 Friday — A planning screen pops up. Confirm your headliner, add a support act, and lock in gear.
💬 Negotiation

When a band replies, open Inbox (📬) and choose: Accept (standard fee), Negotiate (risk a lower fee), Guest List (pay extra, gain REP), or Pass.

🎸 Show Night (Saturday)

The show runs automatically. Spend your weekly budget tokens wisely — bigger bands cost more. Win fans, grow your REP, and unlock bigger venues.

⚠️ Watch Out
  • 💸 If you run out of money, it's game over.
  • 🎲 Backup bands are cheap but risky — they might cause chaos.
  • 📣 Promotions booked mid-week boost attendance on show night.
