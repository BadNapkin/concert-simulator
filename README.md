## 🎭 The Story

You've just signed the lease on **The Dive Bar** — 100 capacity, sticky floors, one spotlight that flickers. You've got £800, a phone full of half-remembered contacts, and a dream of building the most legendary music venue in the city.

Scroll through **BandBook** and swipe on acts you believe in. Build relationships. Book the right band on the right night. Watch the show unfold live on stage. Read the reviews. Survive the rival venues texting to rub it in.

Every booking is a gamble. Every show is a story.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Discover** — Browse BandBook and contact bands that match your vibe
2. **Book** — Assign a headliner (and optional support act) to your weekly show
3. **Run the Show** — Watch the canvas animation as the night unfolds in real time
4. **Read the Review** — *The Static* covers the night; check your revenue and relationship tier
5. **Grow** — Level up your venue, build legendary partnerships, break new acts

### Tips
- 🎯 Band **traits** tell you everything — no stat bars needed
- 💬 Your rivals will text. It's fine. Let them.
- 🔁 Relationship tiers unlock better revenue splits and exclusive bookings
- 📈 A bad show with a great band still builds history — keep booking them

## ⚙️ Technical Details

- **Single file** — entire game in `index.html`, zero build step
- **Canvas 2D** — live show animation with silhouettes, light beams & particle effects
- **Pure JS** — no frameworks, no dependencies, works offline
- **Responsive** — desktop & mobile layout
- **Dark aesthetic** — `#0a0a0f` background, purple glow, Georgia serif

```bash

cd concert-simulator
open index.html   # macOS
# or: start index.html  (Windows)

```

No server needed. Just open and play.
